Over the past year, COVID concentrations in wastewater peaked around February 2024 and subsequently exhibited a gradual decline through September 2024. Flu A concentrations showed a significant increase starting in December 2023, peaking in February 2024, before tapering off and maintaining a relatively low level until September 2024. Flu B concentrations experienced several peaks and fluctuations, with the highest concentration observed in early 2024, followed by a decline and relative stability through the summer months. RSV concentrations began to rise in November 2023 and peaked around January 2024, followed by a notable decrease and steady low levels through September 2024.
